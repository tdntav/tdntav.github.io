HOW TO PLAY
Take a string of letters, make a chain of words.

Select cells from the grid and/or the existing word to make a new word.

PLAYABLE MOVES:
- Replace a word letter with a grid letter,
- insert a grid letter in a slot between letters,
- swap two word letters,
- place a word letter in a slot to rearrange the word.

When a playable selection is made, use the PLAY button to play a valid word.

Grid letters refresh when a row or column is completed.

BONUSES:
Get a bonus no-penalty setup move for every 5 added to your chain.
Play bonus setup moves with the "||" button.

Get a bonus grid refresh for every 30 string earned. Activate bonus grid refresh with the "#" button.

You can expand the grid once by making a six letter word.

Attempting to play a previously played word does not incur a penalty.

Words that fail dictionary validation are penalised with increasing severity.

Five invalid words leads to disqualification.

I know it's sometimes the dictionary's fault and I'm sorry about that.

Enjoy!

ABOUT

Big shoutout to dictionaryapi.dev for providing a free dictionary resource. This is the spirit of random collaboration that embodies the internet at its best.

Without whoever that is, whoever I am couldn't have made this game for whoever you are.

You'll notice the lack of ads and surveillance. Doesn't that make a nice change? Play as much as you like. It's a good game, right?

Once you save up a lot of bonuses, you can challenge yourself to play longer words, to tap into rich veins of word association and rhyme. Aspiring poets and rappers should love this.

But the longer it goes, the harder it gets. Once you've used all the obvious and convenient short words, the game forces you to get creative.
