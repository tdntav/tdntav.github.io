A bonus no-penalty setup move is earned
for every 5 words added to the chain.


To use it, select a move as normal but
hit the BONUS || button instead of PLAY.


A free grid refresh bonus is earned
for every 30 points (AKA string).

GOOD TO KNOW

If no grid refresh bonus is used, grid letters refresh when a row or column is completed.

Attempting to play a previously played word does not incur a penalty.

Words that fail dictionary validation are penalised with increasing severity.

The fifth invalid attempt ends the game. You can see your remaining lives under the PLAY button.

Enjoy!

ABOUT

Big shoutout to dictionaryapi.dev for providing a free dictionary resource. This is the spirit of random collaboration that embodies the internet at its best.

Without whoever that is, whoever I am couldn't have made this game for whoever you are.

Occasionally you might try to play a real word that is inexplicably missing from the dictionary. It will fail validation. I'm sorry about that. The dictionary is amazing but it's not perfect.

Gameplay is what you make it. You have to start off with short words, but once you bank some bonuses, you can challenge yourself to play longer words. Tap into your rich personal lexicon, or just guess at words that are likely to exist.

The game displays definitions to help you explore your own ever-expanding vocabulary.

But the longer it goes, the harder it gets. Once you've used up all the obvious and convenient short words, the game forces you to get creative.
